About the Telemetry Data

The ASPIRE study investigates how university students choose, refine, and persist their account passwords. By surveying common vulnerabilities across large, real-world credential sets, the system produces a behavioural map of what makes a password resilient — and what makes it fail in seconds.

The goal is to guide users toward stronger habits through live feedback, intervene on the riskiest submissions, and measure how participants adapt after seeing a warning. Every row below is a single dataset, an aggregate of millions of individual password choices that fuels the analytics portal.
