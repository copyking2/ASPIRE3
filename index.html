ASPIRE Password Resilience Study

A multi-page research environment that captures how students choose, refine, and persist their account passwords. The system pairs a live participant simulator with a researcher analytics dashboard to track behavioral telemetry in real time.

  • ModeAnonymous · Local-only
  • CaptureEntropy, keystrokes, interventions
  • StorageIn-browser (localStorage)

Sign in to your study account

Sign up or log in to measure the vulnerability and resilience of your personal password habits. Returning participants can review their individual telemetry, while new accounts are onboarded through the same secure study flow.

Researcher portal

Authenticate with your researcher credentials to view the live dashboard: strength distribution, secure adoption rate, intervention outcomes, the Resilience Delta behavioral flow and the submission log.

What the system captures

  • 100-point scoring matrix Length · diversity · pattern · entropy
  • Real-time telemetry Basic properties · conditional alerts · cyber attack simulation
  • Resilience Delta Baseline → intervention → adaptation per session
  • Hardware-aware crack-time Desktop PC · GPU · GPU farm cluster

Data is stored locally in your browser. No information is transmitted to a server. Open the participant simulator in one tab and the researcher portal in another to generate sample submissions.
